Nut Mixes

Eating nuts as part of healthy diet is good for your heart.

Besides protein nuts contain unsaturated fats, Omega3 fatty acids, fibre, vitamin E, plant sterols and L-arginine. This is a substance that helps improve the health of your artery walls by making them more flexible and less prone to blood clots that can block blood flow.
