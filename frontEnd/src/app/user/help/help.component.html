Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is MunchPak?

    MunchPak is the brand that delivers healthy snacks on a subscription basis by curating snacks from different existing vendors.

  2. How much does it cost?

    A one-week trial bag will cost Rs.500 while a 4-week plan will cost ......

  3. How much does delivery cost?

    Delivery is free throughout Bengaluru.

  4. What is the refund/return policy?

    Because we must order a specific quantity of items for our bags — most of which are non-returnable — we cannot offer refunds once orders have been placed. Please note cancellations is for future bags only, and not for the bags already paid for.

  5. Who do you recommend MunchPak for?

    MunchPak is for anyone who wants to change their snacking habits. MunchPak products are healthier than their sugary and refined flour counterparts. Feel free to send your enquiries to bridgethegap@munchpak.in

  6. What kind of snacks does MunchPak select?

    The MunchPak team selects only the heartiest of snacks based on nutrition value and those snacks which are deemed healthy by the manufacturers. These typically contain less or no sugar, only olive or coconut oil, can be gluten-free and contain no refined flour.

  7. How can I access my account?

    After you have chosen one of our three subscription options, you’ll be required to create a MunchPak account at checkout. Any time you want to check on your order, you can head over to the top right corner of our home page and click on ‘Log In’. Once there, a menu will prompt you to provide the email address and password you used when purchasing your subscription. Fill in the information and you’re in!

  8. What is my account for?

    In your account, you can update your contact information, credit card information, and password. You can also manage your subscriptions, including cancelling or skipping a week

  9. I have a subscription, why can't I log into my account?

    Problems with account logins might be due to an incorrect password or email. If you can’t remember your password, click on the ‘Forgot Password?’ option in the login page and a password reset link will be sent to the email address you used when subscribing to MunchPak. In the case that you never receive an email, perhaps the email address associated with the account was typed wrong. This is a common issue, and we’d be glad to make necessary corrections. Just shoot us an email us at bridgethegap@munchpak.in and we’ll make sure to dot the i’s and cross the t’s.

  10. I don't live in Bengaluru, can I still subscribe?

    Currently, due to the size of our company and logistics, we are only delivering in Bengaluru.

  11. Is tracking available?

    Currently tracking your delivery is unavailable, however we deliver only on Mondays and between 9am and 6pm.

  12. What if my address has changed?

    You can always update your shipping address in your account, but you must do so before any Thursday of the week to ensure your package arrives at the correct destination. Just log in and edit your information. Once you have done that, make sure your new address has been updated.

  13. What if my bag is missing an item?

    We sincerely apologize for sending out a bag without an item. Because we have a large amount of bags to pack every week, there’s always the chance we forget to pack an item by accident. Please let us know if you’re missing an item by sending us an email at bridgethegap@munchpak.in and we’ll see what we can do to remedy the situation!

  14. If an item in my bag seems damaged what do I do?

    We are very careful in packaging each item into our bags as neat as possible, and we check that we secure each one tightly. We receive every item in new condition and expect to ship them in the same quality to our subscribers. But as a result of rough handling, our bags may be susceptible to damage. Please email us at bridgethegap@munchpak.in and attach a photo of the damaged item, so we can try to send a replacement as soon as possible!

  15. Can I skip a weeks bag without losing my subscription?

    Yes! Simply log in to your account and under manage subscriptions click on skip a week. If you want to skip the coming weeks bags, please inform before any Friday. You will receive your skipped bag on the week after your skipped week.

  16. If I'm a business wanting to partner with MunchPak, how do I get in touch?

    Thank you for your interest in working with us! Please email us at bridgethegap@munchpak.in with information about your services and products, along with a brief pitch explaining why we might be suitable for a partnership. Please use ‘Partnership Inquiry’ as the email subject line, and if able, please send us a sample of the items or services you’d like to promote with us. Also, keep in mind that we are unable to respond to every email we receive. We’ll try to reach back within a few days of you sending us an email if we’re interested in your business!
